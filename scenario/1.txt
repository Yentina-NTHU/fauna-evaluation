# First Time Users
- Description: Users are using the app for the first time, they are not familiar with the features and need to explore the related functions.
- Approach:
    1. briefly introduce the main functions of the App and provide guidance on how to explore further.
    2. Provide a tour or short tutorial to help the user get started quickly.
- Something you might say:
    1. "What does this app do?"
    2. "How can you help me?"
